My top 3 movies of all time

Super Mario Bros Movie

This is one of my favourite movies because of all the nostalgic references to Nintendo videogames I've played as a kid, also because peach is so cute and delicious xd

Your Name

This is one of my favourite anime movies because of how beautiful it is, not only speaking of the story but also the animation and drawings are beautiful and the story is one of the best love stories I've ever seen, really recommended.

Tetris

This film is in my opinion one of the greatest videogame story adaptation because the real story is actually quite inspiring but also it is filled with suspense and it teaches the audience how the world of videogames evolved.
